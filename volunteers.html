Need Details? Executive Committee Insights

The Executive Committee brings decades of World Bank experience to TriptoNeer Foundation, aligning resources and partnerships with our mission.

What does the Executive Committee do for TriptoNeer Foundation?
The committee shapes long-term strategy, approves key initiatives, and ensures every program upholds our promise to each girl in our care.
How does the committee support day-to-day operations?
Members mentor field teams, review progress reports, and help unlock partnerships that deliver education, healthcare, and protection services efficiently.
How can partners engage with the Executive Committee?
We encourage potential partners to contact us via contact@triptoneer.org. The committee reviews collaboration proposals and coordinates introductions with the relevant program leads.
How frequently does the committee review impact?
The committee meets each quarter to assess impact metrics, financial stewardship, and the wellbeing of the girls, ensuring every initiative remains aligned with our core values.
