Who We Are A Home Where OVGs Can Flourish

Tripto Neer Foundation (TnF) exists to ensure orphaned and vulnerable girls in Bangladesh grow up with stability, dignity, and hope. We provide a family-style home where education, wellbeing, and emotional care are under one roof. Our approach is simple: meet the needs of today while preparing each girl to shape her own tomorrow.

From boarding school enrollment to everyday mentorship, we invest in each girl’s whole journey. Our supporters, volunteers, and team members are united by one promise—every contribution fuels a single fund that adapts to the most urgent needs of the girls we serve.

What Guides Us Principles Behind Every Decision

Each girl who joins our family arrives with a different story. Our guiding principles help us remain responsive, consistent, and accountable as we care for them.

Holistic Care

We combine safe housing, nutritious meals, health services, and personal coaching to create stability.

Education First

Boarding school placements and tutoring support unlock academic pathways and future careers.

Family Culture

We cultivate a nurturing environment grounded in respect, empathy, and shared responsibility.

Accountability

One unified fund gives us flexibility while ensuring transparent reporting to every donor and partner.

Transparency Stewardship You Can Trust

Our promise to donors is simple: your support powers every aspect of a girl’s life at Tripto Neer. We track expenses carefully, review progress monthly, and share updates with our community so you always know the impact you are making.

  • Unified fund management with quarterly board reviews
  • Regular impact briefs shared with donors and partners
  • Independent audits and child safeguarding protocols
