Welcome to Tripto Neer Foundation Small Actions Lead to Big Changes

Tripto Neer Foundation (TnF) is a compassionate organization dedicated to supporting orphaned and vulnerable girls (OVGs). We provide comprehensive care, including food, accommodation, healthcare, and education, ensuring their safety, well-being, and future success. The name "Tripto" means "satisfied," symbolizing our mission to create a nurturing family environment for girls in need.

Our Mission Safe, loving, and supportive home for OVGs

Our mission is to offer a safe, loving, and supportive home for OVGs, meeting their fundamental needs and helping them grow into empowered individuals. Through quality education in boarding schools, healthcare, and emotional support, we strive to provide a foundation for them to achieve their dreams and contribute positively to society.
